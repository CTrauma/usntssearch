Connection to SICKBEARD
Sickbeard connectivity is always active. No configurations are needed on NZBMegasearch.
In Sickbeard: go to Config/Search Providers and add a custom Newznab provider, no apikey needed.
See below for an example with a locally configured NZBMegasearch with port 5000.
Enjoy.

Connection to COUCHPOTATO
Couchpotato connectivity is always active. No configurations are needed on NZBMegasearch.
In Couchpotato: go to Settings/Providers and add a custom Newznab provider, no apikey needed.
See below for an example with a locally configured NZBMegasearch with port 5000.
Enjoy.
